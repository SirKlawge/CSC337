Things You'll Need:

  • Tools
    • An oven :)
    • One cookie sheet
    • One sauce pan
    • One bowl
    • Pie weights
    • Parchment paper
    • Whisk
    • Spatula
  • Ingredients
    • 1 ready-to-bake 9.5" pie shell
    • 1 stick of butter
    • 1/2 cup of white sugar
    • 1 cup of light brown sugar
    • 1/2 cup of light corn syrup
    • 3 tbsp of flour
    • 2 tbsp of milk
    • 1/2 tsp of fine salt
    • 1 tsp of vanilla extract
    • 1 tbsp of bourbon
    • 3 large eggs

Preliminary: Begin by laying the pecan halves evenly on the cookie sheet.

Step 1: Blind Baking Process

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F
  2. Lay the parchment paper over the pie shell. Push it down in the center a little.
  3. Pour the in the pie weights evenly onto the parchment paper to weigh it down.
  4. Once the oven has reached 350°F, prebake the pie shell by setting it in the oven for 20 minutes
  5. Remove the pie shell from the oven and remove the parchment and pie weights from it.
  6. Prebake the pie shell for 10 more minutes without the parchment and pie weights.
  7. With 7-8 minutes left, toast the pecans by adding them to the oven on their own shelf.

Step 2: Prepare the Filling

  1. Grab your sauce pan and add to it (all the same quantites as listed under Ingredients):
    • 1 stick of butter
    • 1/2 cup of white sugar
    • 1 cup of light brown sugar
    • 1/2 cup of light corn syrup
    • 3 tbsp of flour
    • 2 tbsp of milk
    • 1/2 tsp of fine salt
    • 1 tsp of vanilla extract
    • 1 tbsp of bourbon
    So pretty much all of the ingredients but the eggs!
  2. Put the sauce pan on the stove on medium heat. Bring the sauce to a boil: whisking as it melts.
  3. When large bubbles start to form in the center, turn off the heat. Let it cool on stove for 10 minutes.
  4. While the sauce pan is cooling, crack three large eggs into a bowl and whisk them.
  5. After 10 minutes of cooling, gradually add the contents of the sauce pan to the bowl of eggs while continuing to whisk.

Step 3: Assenble the Prebaked Pie

  1. Add the pecans evenly to the pie shell.
  2. Pour the filling into the pie shell until it's nearly at the top.
  3. With the spatula, tap the surface of the pecans to ensure they're coated with the filling.

Step 4: Bake!

  1. Place in the center of the oven at 350°F for 40-45 minutes.
  2. After you remove it, give it a little shake. There should be no wiggle in the center. If there is, put it back in the oven for a few minuites.
  3. Let it cool completely before cutting into it and serving!
